About Essy Properties

Welcome to Essy Properties — your trusted real estate partner nestled in the heart of Ruiru. We're here to simplify the journey of buying and selling properties with elegance, transparency, and heart.

Who We Are

Essy Properties is a locally rooted, superuser-managed real estate platform designed to serve all property seekers across Ruiru and beyond. We're not just agents — we’re matchmakers for homes and hearts.

What We Do

We list and showcase top-notch properties for sale. From elegant houses to spacious plots, every listing is uploaded by our verified seller to ensure you’re seeing the best of what’s available.

Why Choose Us?

With a clean, user-friendly interface and features like favorites, seller messaging, meeting requests, and mobile optimization — we bring modern real estate to your fingertips.

One Seller, Personalized Service

All properties on this platform are uploaded and managed by one trusted seller (our superuser), ensuring consistency, direct communication, and top-tier service at every step.
